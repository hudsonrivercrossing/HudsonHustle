Suggested reading: Fraunces + Inter is the strongest `v2.1` candidate if the goal is editorial shell energy with practical in-game readability. Cormorant + Inter is more romantic and more obviously “designerly,” but also easier to overuse. Sora + Inter is the safest fallback if the editorial route ends up feeling too soft for the product. If you want clear separation from default vibe-coded product typography, compare `Manrope + IBM Plex Sans`, `Space Grotesk + IBM Plex Sans`, and `Archivo Black + DM Sans` most closely.
